FNaF 1: Five Nights at Freddy's (2014) - survive Freddy Fazbear's Pizza as night guard.

FNaF 2: Toy animatronics and facial recognition introduced. Player is Jeremy Fitzgerald.

FNaF 3: Security guard at Fazbear's Fright, facing Springtrap. Audio/ventilation mechanics.

FNaF 4: Player is a child in their bedroom, fend off nightmare animatronics.

SL (FNaF 5 - Sister Location): Last game the user played. Circus Baby’s Entertainment, Michael Afton, dynamic room-to-room gameplay, multiple endings.

FNaF 6: Pizza Simulator - run a pizzeria, survive animatronics after hours.

UCN (FNaF 7 - Ultimate Custom Night): 50 animatronics, ultimate customization, survive until 6 AM.

FNaF: Security Breach (FNaF 9): Gregory navigates Freddy Fazbear's Mega Pizzaplex, stealth and exploration.

FNaF Movie (2023): Live-action adaptation, horror elements, Freddy Fazbear's Pizza central.

FNaF Movie 2 (upcoming): Sequel to 2023 movie, expanded storyline.

- FNaF Rewritten: Fan remake with expanded story.

- FNaF Dark Carnival: Fan-made survival horror, unique animatronics.
- FNaF Horror Nights: Fan mod with multiplayer elements.
